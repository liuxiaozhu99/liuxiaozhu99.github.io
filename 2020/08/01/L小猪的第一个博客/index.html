My First Blog

Hello! Glad for your clicking on my blog！

And this is my fisrt try at writting a passage, with the grammar of markdown. I would never tell you how clumsy I am when writting this passage.

Writting while searching for the documents of mardown… And this kind of state becomes more and more common in my daily life.

As for computer science and technology, you always have many things to learn, which is also the first contact with it for you and very difficult.

WE HAVE TO LEARN IT, AND DO IT ON OUR OWN!!!

I have to say that it is a big challenge that I’ve never meet. But I hope I can be filled with bravery, pull through and enjoy it,making it a part of life.

Cause it’ really a enjoyable happiness when you get through all this difficultis and see the world you build with code on your own hands with your own eyes.

No matter what I meet in my common life, happiness or saddness, I wish I could always find happiness in the coding world and recording in this blog.

Thank you for your reading！

我的第一篇博客

Hello! 很高兴您能点击进入 L小猪 的博客！

这也是俺第一次用markdown的语法写博客哩！别指望我会告诉你，写这篇博客的时候有多难。。。

TM一边写，一边在网上翻markdown的文档… 而且这种状态在日常生活中变得越来越常见。

对于计算机科学嘛，总是有好多好多东西要学呢，而且这些知识都是第一次接触且有些艰深难懂。

自学！躬行！

不得不说，这是先前从未遇见的巨大挑战，可仍希望能怀满勇气，直面并克服困难，最终享受于其中，令其为生活之组分。

因为当你度过了所有的困难，并且用 你亲眼看到你用自己的双手构建的世界 ，这真的是一种享受的幸福。

无论我在平常的生活中遇到什么，快乐还是悲伤，我都希望我能在代码的世界以及在博客记录中找到快乐。

感谢您的阅读！
